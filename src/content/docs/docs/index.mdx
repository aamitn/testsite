## What is WinHider?

**WinHider** is a lightweight, privacy-first tool that empowers you to **instantly hide specific windows** during screen sharing. Whether you're sharing during a meeting, presentation, or livestream—WinHider ensures confidential apps and documents stay private.

<br/>
## Why Professionals Use WinHider

- 🧑‍💼 **Consultants** use it to prevent clients from seeing sensitive tools.
- 🏫 **Teachers** keep personal chats hidden during remote classes.
- 💼 **Executives** protect internal dashboards during presentations.
